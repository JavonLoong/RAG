Sichuan–Shanghai gas pipeline () is a 1702 km long natural gas pipeline in China.
 The pipeline runs from Pugang gas field in Dazhou, Sichuan Province, to Qingpu District of Shanghai.
 An 842 km long branch line connects Yichang in Hubei with Puyang in Henan Province.
 Two shorter branches are located near the Puguang gas field and one in the east near Shanghai.
Tinghu District () is one of three districts of Yancheng, Jiangsu province, China.
 (The other two are Yandu District and Dafeng District).
 Prior to 2004, Tinghu District was called the Urban District ()of Yancheng.
Dafeng () is a coastal district under the administration of Yancheng, Jiangsu province, China.
 Located on the Jiangsu North Plain with a coastline of 112 km , Dafeng was historically one of the largest salt-making areas in China and now is famed for its well preserved eco-system and numerous national conservation parks.
 The district has the largest national nature reserve for a rare deer species, Père David's Deer or Milu (麋鹿 ) in Chinese.
 It borders the prefecture-level city of Taizhou to the southwest.
