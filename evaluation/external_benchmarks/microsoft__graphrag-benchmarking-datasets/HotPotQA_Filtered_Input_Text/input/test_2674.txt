The 1980 Mongolian National Championship was the sixteenth recorded edition of the Mongolian National Championship for football, with the first tournament taking place in 1955 and no tournament held in 1965 or apparently in 1977.
 The 1980 national championship was won by Aldar (literally "Glory"; a team representing the Army sports society) their fourth recorded title, following their victory in the 1970 championship.
 Though it would appear however that championships were contested between 1956 and 1963, as sources note that a team called Aldar, the Mongolian Army Sports Club, won the title on numerous occasions during that time.

Harry Hyde (January 17, 1925 – May 13, 1996) was a leading crew chief in NASCAR stock car racing in the 1960s through the 1980s, winning 56 races and 88 pole positions.
 He was the 1970 championship crew chief for Bobby Isaac.
 He inspired the Harry Hogge character in the movie "Days of Thunder".
