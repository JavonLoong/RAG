The Shillong Premier League is an association football league in India.
 It started with 8 teams in 2012.
 Royal Wahingdoh F.C. clinched the title for the third consecutive year after winning the previous two editions in 2010 and 2011.

Ngouba Singh (born 1 March 1995 in Manipur) is an Indian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Royal Wahingdoh F.C. in the I-League.
Royal Wahingdoh Football Club is a men's professional football club based in Shillong, Meghalaya that participates in the Indian I-League.
 Royal Wahingdoh F.C. holds the record for the highest number of consecutive Shillong Premier League titles, having won the finals in December 2010, 2011 and in 2012.
 They played in I-League 2nd Division for the 2013-14 season.
 They won the league by defeating Bhowanipore F.C. in the last match of the league and qualified to play for the 2014-15 season of the I-League.
