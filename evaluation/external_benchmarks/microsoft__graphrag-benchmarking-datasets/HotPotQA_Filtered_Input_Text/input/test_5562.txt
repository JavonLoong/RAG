Sir John de Seton was a Scottish knight who took part in the War of Scottish Independence, as a supporter of Robert de Brus.
 He held lands in England and Scotland.
Sir William Wallace (Scottish Gaelic: "Uilleam Uallas" ] ; Norman French: "William le Waleys"; died 23 August 1305) was a Scottish knight who became one of the main leaders during the Wars of Scottish Independence.
Sir David de Inchmartin was a Scottish knight who took part in the War of Scottish Independence, as a supporter of Robert de Brus.
Sir William Oliphant (died aft.
 1313), was a Scottish knight and Governor of Stirling Castle during the Wars of Scottish Independence.
 He switched loyalties to the English and died in a Scottish prison.
Sir William Munro of Foulis (died 1505) was a Scottish Knight and Scottish clan chief of the highland Clan Munro.
 He is by tradition the 12th Baron of Foulis and 15th overall chief of the clan.
 However, he is actually only the 5th chief of the Clan Munro who can be proved by contemporary evidence.
Sir William Keith of Galston (d.1336) was a Scottish Knight who fought in the Wars of Scottish Independence.
Sir Reginald Crawford (died 1307) was a Scottish knight who took part in the Wars of Scottish Independence.

Sir David de Brechin (died 1320) was a Scottish knight who fought on both sides during the Wars of Scottish Independence.
