The Warroad Lakers were an American Senior ice hockey team from Warroad, Minnesota.
 The Lakers played in various Manitoba AHA and Thunder Bay AHA senior and intermediate leagues and were granted special eligibility for the Allan Cup and Hardy Cup by the Canadian Amateur Hockey Association.
